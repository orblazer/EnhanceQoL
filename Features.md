# EnhanceQoL Features

## Core Module

### Chat
- **Enable chat fading** – allow chat messages to fade after a delay.
- **Enable Instant Messenger** – open whispers in a compact IM-style window.
- **Fade Instant Messenger when unfocused** – reduce opacity when not active.
- **Enable Raider.IO link in Context Menu** – add a context menu entry to copy the sender's Raider.IO profile URL.
- **Enable Warcraftlogs link in Context Menu** – add a WarcraftLogs URL to the context menu.
- **Use custom whisper sound** – play a custom sound for new messages.
- **Hide Instant Messenger in combat** – suppress the IM window and sound while in combat.
- **Animate window** – slide the IM window when showing or hiding.

### Bags & Inventory
- **Display item level on the Merchant Frame.**
- **Display item level on equipment in all bags.**
- **Item level label position in bags** – choose where the text appears on bag icons.
- **Item level label position on Character Frame** – choose where the text appears on equipment slots.
- **Enable item filter in bags** – drag with <kbd>Shift</kbd> to move.
- **Enable money tracker** – track gold across all characters and show Warband gold.
- **Display item level on the Bank Frame.**
- **Display bind type on bag items** – show BoE/WuE/WB in addition to item level.
- **Fade profession quality icons during search** – dim quality icons while filtering.
- **Show account gold only** – only display Warband gold (hidden coins and copper).

### Character & Inspect
- **Display item level on Character Equipment Frame.**

- **Hide Order Hall Command Bar.**
- **Show additional information on the Inspect Frame.**
- **Display Catalyst charges on Character Frame.**
- **Enable Gem-Socket Helper.**
- Class-specific toggles such as **Hide Rune Frame** or **Hide Combo Point Bar** appear for the corresponding class.

### Action Bars & Mouse
- **Mouseover action bars** – show each bar only on mouseover.
- **Full button range coloring** – tint the entire action button when out of range.

### Unit Frames
- **Hide floating combat text over your character.**
- **Hide floating combat text over your pet.**
- Options to hide Target, Player or Boss frames until mouseover.
- **Truncate unit names** with adjustable maximum name length.
- **Enable frame scale adjustment** – slider to change size of compact unit frames.

- **Hide Minimap Button.**
- **Hide Bagsbar.**
- **Hide Micro Menu.**
- **Hide Quick Join Toast.**
- **Hide Raid Tools in Party.**
